Two principles defend against every file above

Least privilege

Scope OAuth grants, agent tools, tokens and admin accounts to the minimum. Every excess permission is a gift to whoever takes it.

Trust nothing unverified

Anything an AI reads, a user supplies, or a vendor ships is untrusted until checked — including the AI's own words to your customers.

The defenses are mostly boring and cheap — change a default password, apply a known patch, verify an MFA reset, pin a dependency, confirm before an irreversible action. The failures are catastrophic and expensive. That asymmetry is the whole argument for doing the basics well.
